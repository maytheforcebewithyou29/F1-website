Information:

  • Formula 1 (F1) is the world's top level of car racing, featuring ultra-fast, high-tech single-seater cars competing in a series of international Grand Prix's. Each season has 24 races, all around the world elite drivers from teams like Ferrari, Mercedes, and Mclaren race on circuits across the globe—from city streets to purpose-built tracks—earning points based on their finishing positions. The driver and team with the most points at the end of the season are crowned World Champions. F1 combines speed, strategy, and cutting-edge engineering, with fans drawn to the drama of overtakes, pit stops, rivalries, and the constant pursuit of performance
